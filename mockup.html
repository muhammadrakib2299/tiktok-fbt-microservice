FBT lives inside the existing tiktok-microservice as a new fbt/ submodule. The merchant chooses fulfilment modes per TikTok shop via two independent toggles (fbm_enabled · fbt_enabled) on the Accounts page. The "TikTok Shop" sidebar entry renders one of three views accordingly: General-only (today's default), FBT-only, or Hybrid. FBT orders live on their own page and are excluded from both the central order list and the picker queue.

Key insight: there is no separate "FBT account" to connect. Each TikTok shop carries two independent fulfilment-mode flags (fbm_enabled · fbt_enabled); at least one must stay ON (DB constraint chk_accounts_any_mode). Flipping FBT ON triggers a verification call to TikTok; flipping FBM OFF is a one-click conversion (no API check). The sidebar reshapes to match — General-only, FBT-only, or Hybrid — within one render tick.
Conditional sidebar · three views

One TikTok Shop entry, three possible shapes

Two flags drive the menu: fbm_enabled (seller-fulfilled, default ON) and fbt_enabled (default OFF) — each aggregated across all of the client's TikTok accounts. The "Accounts" entry is always rendered because that's where both toggles live; everything else depends on which modes the client uses. The change happens in one render tick after any toggle flips — no reload.

What happens on enable: verification API call to TikTok → if eligible: UPDATE accounts SET fbt_enabled=true → guard canPublishFbt() checks both fbt_enabled and publish_fbt_to_wms (fail-closed; ops kill-switch borrowed from Amazon FBA) → publish tiktok_fbt.to.wms.account_fbt_enabled → core-service creates virtual warehouse "TikTok FBT UK" → first FBT inventory poll queued → sidebar re-renders with FBT items visible.

Rare state — admin has paused FBT publishing

accounts.publish_fbt_to_wms is an admin-only kill-switch (default TRUE). When ops flips it to FALSE — typically during a downstream consumer outage — the merchant's toggle stays ON but no RabbitMQ messages flow. The card shows an amber banner; merchants take no action. Pattern adopted from Amazon FBA's publish_fba_to_wms after a 421-order fail-open incident (see LESSONS.md in amazon-microservice).

⚠ FBT publishing temporarily paused
Your shop is enabled, but our team has paused data flow to your WMS while we resolve a downstream issue. New FBT orders and inventory updates will resume once paused is lifted — no action needed from you. Contact support if this banner is still here after 24 hours.
